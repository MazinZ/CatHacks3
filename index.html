Episode 4: A New Hack

In April 2015, CatHacks was the first national college hackathon in the state of Kentucky. This year, we're back for round three.
On April 7, 2018, hackers will travel from colleges across the nation to the University of Kentucky in Lexington. You'll have 24 hours to explore new technologies, meet talented students, and network with innovative tech companies. The food is on us, and so is the swag; CatHacks is completely free!

Check-in starts at 10AM on April 17th. View the full schedule below.

Eligibility and Travel

In order to attend CatHacks, you must be a student at a post-secondary university. We accept high school students on a case by case basis. Even if you don't have much experience, you should still apply!
We're offering travel reimbursement to as many participants as possible. Let us know if you need some help, and we'll do our best to get you here.


By applying, you agree to the Code of Conduct.

Hardware

We will be providing our own Hardware Lab, so there will be some hardware to check out and use at the event! Arduinos, VR headsets, Amazon Alexas, and more will be available for use.

Sponsor

CatHacks would not be possible without the support of our sponsors. These companies allow us to keep CatHacks a free and open event for our participants, who value the opportunity to network with industry leaders.
