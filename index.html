The epic trilogy continues.

In April 2015, CatHacks was the first national college hackathon in the state of Kentucky. This year, we're back for round three.
On April 15th-16th, 2017, hackers will travel from colleges across the nation to the University of Kentucky in Lexington. You'll have 24 hours to explore new technologies, meet talented students, and network with innovative tech companies. The food is on us, and so is the swag; CatHacks is completely free!

See you at CatHacks III.

Eligibility and Travel

In order to attend CatHacks, you must be a student at a post-secondary university. We accept high school students on a case by case basis. Even if you don't have much experience, you should still apply!
We're offering travel reimbursement to as many participants as possible. Let us know if you need some help, and we'll do our best to get you here.


By applying, you agree to the Code of Conduct.

Hardware

Major League Hacking will be providing a Hardware Lab, so there will be tons of hardware to check out and use at the event! Arduinos, Oculus DK, Myo, Pebble, and Laptops are only some of the items that will be available.
